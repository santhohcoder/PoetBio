Kazi Nazrul Islam

The national poet of bangladesh

Rebel poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of bangladesh who is especially recognised as a Bidrohi Kobi(rebel poet) around the country. Nazrul us a famous poet, musician and philosopher of Bangladesh. The famous poet Nazrul's writing deal with the revolution of that period of the British government. His revolutionar attempt heloed him to be a national poet if Bangladesh. he has raised his voice against any kinds of fascism and oppression and got popularity swiftly around the Indian Subcontinent.

Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writing explored themes such as love,freedom,humanity and revolutinn. He oppressed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism including religious, caste-based and gender-based. Throughout his career, Nazrul wrote short stories,novels and essays but is best known for his songs and poems. He pioneered new music forms such as Bengalu ghazals. Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs(many recorded on HMV and gramopj=hine records), collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.
